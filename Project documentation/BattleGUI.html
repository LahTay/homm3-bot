BattleGUI.attack_enemy(x: int, y: int, direction: str)[source]¶

Attacks the enemy on the given coordinates from a given direction. Possible directions are: ‘top’, ‘top_right’, ‘right’, ‘bottom_right’, ‘bottom’, ‘bottom_left’, ‘left’, ‘top_left’ Works the same way as move_to_tile with additional direction

Parameters:

  • x (int) – x coord

  • y (int) – y coord

  • direction (str) – direction we want to attack from

BattleGUI.cast_spell_battle(id)[source]¶

Choose a spell. Its id is its position in the book, starting from 0, and not within the overall magic (If you have 3 spells of tier 1, 2 and 3 then tier 3 spell has id=2, because it lies on the 3rd position in the book) The spell is not played on the battlefield, its only chosen.

Parameters:

id – (int) spell id
